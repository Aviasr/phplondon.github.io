In addition to PHP, I use a number of tools on a day to day basis to debug and QA my work. This talk is an overview of these tools, what they do and how they fit together. The tools I'll be demoing are: Charles Proxy, XDebug, Xhprof and JMeter. I will also demo integrating these with Behat and PHPUnit.
